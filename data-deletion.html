Data Deletion Instructions

Last updated: October 25, 2025

How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your Playyback account and all associated data at any time. Here's how:

Method 1: Through the App

  1. Open the Playyback app
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Tap Account Settings
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account
  5. Confirm your decision

Method 2: Contact Us Directly

If you cannot access the app, you can request data deletion by contacting us:

  • Email: privacy@playyback.com
  • Subject: "Data Deletion Request"
  • Include: Your Facebook email address used for login

What Gets Deleted

When you delete your account, we will remove:

  • Your profile information
  • All game history and scores
  • All connections and friend data
  • All chat messages and conversations
  • All app usage data

Data Deletion Timeline

Your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days of your request. Some data may be retained for legal or security purposes as required by law.

Facebook Data

Deleting your Playyback account does not delete your Facebook account or Facebook data. To delete Facebook data, you must do so through Facebook's settings.

Need Help?

If you have questions about data deletion, contact us:

Email: privacy@playyback.com

Response Time: Within 48 hours
